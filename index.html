goNtwana is an online learning platform for grade 10 -12, aiming to make learning fun and less overwhelming. It is structured by enthusiasts and students who like to spread knowledge and promote education.

We offer free online lessions for all our subjects having quizzes at the end of each and every lesson to test understanding and equip students for the following lesson.

As goNtwana team, we have collected grade 10 -12 previous question papers and also added a feature to allow everyone to either download the question paper or attempt it online and get marked automatically, isn' t that awesome?
