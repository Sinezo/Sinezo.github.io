    • What is Electrical Design?
        Electrical design is the process of creating a detailed plan for the installation and operation of electrical systems in a building or facility. This includes the design of lighting, power distributiom, and other electrical systems.
    • Why is electrical design important
        Electrical design is crucial to ensure the safe and efficient operation of electrical systems. A well-designed electrical system can prevent electrical hazards, reduce energy consumption, and improve overall performance.
    • What are the key components of an electrical design?
        The key components of an electrical design include load analysis, circuit design, equipment selection, wiring and cable design, and safety measures.
    • What software is used for electrical design?
        There are several software programs used for electrical design, including AutoCAD Electrical, Revit MEP, and ETAP. But the softwares we are currently at escaletech are: Arduino IDE, Multism, and Proteus
    • What are the steps in the electrical design process?
        The electrical design process typically includes the following steps: initial site survey, load analysis, circuit design, equipment selection, wiring and cable design, and final design documentation.
